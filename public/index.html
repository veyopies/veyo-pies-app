About Veyo Pies

A Southern Utah Tradition

The history of pies in Veyo, Utah goes back over 30 years, when Evelyn Daniels owned the "Veyo Merc" with her husband Larry. She started making pies as a way to bring more customers to their store — and what started as a labor of love has grown into one of Southern Utah's most beloved bakeries.

Every pie is made from scratch using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients. From our classic fruit pies to our legendary Veyo Volcano, each creation is baked with care and pride right here in Veyo.

Whether you're a longtime local or just passing through on your way to explore the beautiful landscapes of Southern Utah, we invite you to stop by and taste what makes Veyo Pies special.
